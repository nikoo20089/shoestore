Our History

Nike started in 1964 as Blue Ribbon Sports (BRS), founded by Phil Knight and Bill Bowerman. Key milestones include the Waffle Trainer, rebranding to Nike in 1971 with the Swoosh, signing Michael Jordan in 1984, and launching the "Just Do It" slogan in 1988.

Our Family

Nike, Inc. is a global company founded by Phil Knight. The Knight family maintains significant control. Key personnel include Executive Chairman Mark Parker, CEO Elliott Hill, and Chief Financial Officer Matthew Friend. The company also owns Converse.
